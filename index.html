Free Tournament Management Software — Fixturday

Fixturday is free sports tournament management software for football, futsal, basketball, and other team sports. Create a tournament in minutes: add your teams, generate an automatic schedule, and share a live public page with participants. No spreadsheets, no manual standings updates.

What you get for free

  • Automatic schedule generation — round-robin, knockout, and group stage formats
  • Real-time standings calculated after every result
  • Online team registration with organiser approval
  • Public tournament page for participants — mobile-friendly, no login required
  • Multi-pitch scheduling with automatic time slot generation
  • Player roster management per team
  • Print-ready match day schedule

Fixturday is used by sports clubs and tournament organisers to run football tournaments, futsal cups, youth leagues, and local competitions. Free forever — no subscription, no hidden fees.
